# HydroStep 💧🚶

HydroStep is a premium Chrome Extension designed to keep you hydrated and active throughout your workday. Featuring a sleek Apple Watch-inspired Activity Rings UI and native macOS/iOS aesthetics, it seamlessly tracks your water intake and hourly steps.

## Features ✨

- **🍎 Apple Watch Activity Rings UI:** Concentric rings track your 💧 Water (Outer Blue) and 👟 Steps (Inner Purple).
- **⚙️ Custom Working Hours & Goals:** Tailor your schedule (e.g., 9 AM - 5 PM) and daily targets perfectly to your needs.
- **⏰ Smart Timers:** Intelligent alarms that reset exactly 60 minutes after your last logged activity, removing rigid pings.
- **🔥 Daily Streaks:** Stay motivated! A dynamic streak badge tracks every day you conquer both your water and step goals.
- **🌒 Premium Dark Theme:** Glassmorphic elements, vibrant gradients, and San Francisco typography.
- **📊 7-Day Local History:** Securely stores your last 7 days of hydration and activity directly in your browser.
- **↩️ Undo Actions:** Accidentally logged a glass? No problem, instantly undo actions with the built-in `-` buttons.

## Installation 💿

Since this extension is locally packed, you can install it in Chrome Developer Mode:

1. Clone or download this repository to your local machine.
2. Open Google Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Enable **Developer mode** using the toggle switch in the top right corner.
4. Click the **Load unpacked** button in the top left corner.
5. Select the `HydroStep` repository folder.
6. Pin the extension to your Chrome toolbar for easy access!

## Privacy 🔒
HydroStep values your privacy. It requires no accounts, no server connections, and all history tracking is stored securely offline using the `chrome.storage.local` API.
