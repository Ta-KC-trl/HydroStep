# TAKCTRL - Hydro step 💧🚶

![TAKCTRL - Hydro step Banner](banner.png)

### Mini Health AI for Developers & Desk Workers

**TAKCTRL - Hydro step** is a premium Chrome Extension designed to keep you hydrated and active during focused coding sessions. It's more than a reminder; it's your personal health assistant inside Chrome.

---

## 🔥 Features

- **🎯 Smart Interval Reminders:** Set custom intervals for 💧 Water and 👟 Movement that work around your schedule.
- **✨ Apple Watch-Inspired UI:** Dynamic activity rings that visualize your daily progress with smooth animations.
- **🛡️ Streak Tracker:** Stay motivated! A dynamic streak badge tracks every day you conquer both your goals.
- **🌒 Premium Glassmorphic Design:** A sleek, modern dark mode with vibrant gradients and Inter typography.
- **🔒 Privacy First:** No tracking, no accounts, and all data is stored securely offline in your browser.

## ⚙️ Customization

In the **Settings** tab, you can easily control:
- **Water Interval:** How often you want to be reminded to drink (mins).
- **Walk Interval:** How often you should stand up and stretch (mins).
- **Daily Goals:** Set your target cups of water and number of walk sessions.

---

## 💿 Installation

1.  Clone or download this repository.
2.  Open **Google Chrome** and go to `chrome://extensions/`.
3.  Enable **Developer mode** (top right toggle).
4.  Click **Load unpacked** and select the `HydroStep` folder.
5.  Pin it to your toolbar! 📌
